About us

This website provides information on the best coffee shops and work spaces in London. Each venue has been personally vetted by our team at Coffi. We have designed this site with students in mind, and therefore have chosen a variety of venues to match different needs. We understand that student working hours do not conform the the usual 9-5 opening times of cafes, so therefore we have found study venues with better opening hours for students to take advantage of.

The Coffi team:

Hi, I am Izzy! Having lived in London almost all my life I have had plenty of time to seek out the best working venues in London. Having just graduated myself, I understand the daily stresses that university and exam term can bring to students, therefore I hope this website can play a role in reducing this stress.
